What is WebApps Suska?

WebApps Suska is a lab squad that focuses on website deployment-based application development, learning about system requirements analysis, UI/UX Design, Coding Implementation to deploying applications to servers (hosting) With Case Studies.

Learn

Learn about website development and gain new skills through hands-on workshops, events, talks, and project-building activities - both online and in-person.

Grow

Apply the new lessons you get. Improve your career skills, and network. Interact and share knowledge in the community.

Connect

Meet new friends who are interested in programming, and broaden your horizons.
